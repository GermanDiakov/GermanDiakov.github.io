AZNUR specialises in the low-price rental of premium class vehicles. In our branches in Berlin and Stuttgart we offer our sophisticated customers a secure vehicle with high-class equipment that can be booked flexibly and reliably via our website.

Unrestricted mobility at the highest level

Whether company clients, tourists or private persons – individual mobility is a matter of course. With their high quality fittings and driving quality, the luxury vehicles of the AZNUR fleet, predominantly from manufacturer Mercedes-Benz, provide comfort, security and much driving pleasure.

Affordable luxury
